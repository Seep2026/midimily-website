# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Midimily serves two overlapping groups.

- Enterprise owners, business leaders, operators, product teams, customer service teams, and small to mid-sized companies exploring practical AI adoption.
- Individual professionals, students, product managers, operators, programmers, and people who want to turn AI from scattered tool usage into a durable learning and work capability.

Visitors arrive with real uncertainty: what AI can actually change, where to start, how to avoid expensive experiments, and how to turn ideas into a first working loop.

## Product Purpose

Midimily is a brand and service website for "企业的 AI 落地伙伴，个体的 AI 成长顾问".

The site should help visitors understand two service lines:

- Enterprise AI implementation: move AI into real business scenarios, workflows, tools, training, and ongoing support.
- Individual AI growth: build personal AI workflows for learning, work, career change, and portfolio accumulation.

Success means a visitor can quickly choose a path, read a solution deck, understand the first action, and feel safe enough to start a conversation.

## Brand Personality

Clear, warm, and pragmatic.

The voice is calm and specific. It explains AI without hype, reduces anxiety without pretending change is easy, and uses short Chinese sentences that point to action. The brand should feel like a careful partner: professional enough for enterprise owners, approachable enough for individuals who are still learning.

## Anti-references

Midimily should not look or sound like:

- Cyberpunk AI pages with black backgrounds, neon glow, hard grids, or sci-fi dashboards.
- Traditional consulting websites with navy and gold, stock business photography, and generic strategy language.
- Template SaaS landing pages with oversized hero gradients, repeated icon cards, and vague productivity claims.
- Fear-driven AI content that manufactures urgency or talks down to users.
- Public pages with PDF download, PPT download, export, login, CMS, or backend entry points.

Avoid copy such as "赋能", "颠覆", "开启未来", "全栈解决方案", and other broad claims that do not name a concrete action.

## Design Principles

1. Start from a real loop.
   The interface should repeatedly point users toward one concrete scenario, one workflow, one experiment, or one next conversation.

2. Make AI legible.
   Explain AI through paths, decks, service entrances, examples, and small systems rather than abstract technical spectacle.

3. Keep warmth inside professionalism.
   The site should be trustworthy and calm, but not cold. Subtle softness, companion cues, and the 小米立 pet can carry that feeling.

4. Let decks become the proof.
   The solution library and Web Deck pages should feel like a branded product surface, not a blog index or a file download area.

5. Reduce before decorating.
   Remove repeated titles, long explanations, heavy cards, and noisy backgrounds before adding new visual effects.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for public pages.

- Body text should meet 4.5:1 contrast.
- Interactive targets should be at least 44px on mobile.
- The site must not rely on color alone for selected states.
- Motion should be subtle and respect reduced motion preferences.
- Mobile reading must avoid horizontal scroll and compressed Web Deck panels.
- Chinese copy should use natural line breaks, short sentences, and avoid dense paragraph blocks.
